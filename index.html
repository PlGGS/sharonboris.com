Welcome to Sharon Boris, LCSW, LLC! I am pleased to offer you a supportive and collaborative environment.

Choose from a wide variety of time tested methods, and we will work together to custom tailor a plan suited to your individual needs. It's all up to you!

We can use one or a combination of approaches. Whatever suits you best.



I received a BS degree Magna Cum Laude with a double major in biology and psychology and went on to receive a Master of Social Work degree from Loyola University Chicago.

I've worked in counseling programs, holistic medical centers, and teaching hospitals. With that 20 years experience of continually applying different kinds of appraoches to my work, I'll make it easy for us to work together and choose the best approaches to suit your needs.

Throughout my work, I make use of a set of different theoretical modalities that I've immersed myself in over the years. They'll allows us to more easily put together the best plan for you.

We'll make use of Supportive Counseling, Psychodynamic Theory, Cognitive Therapy, and Solution-focused approaches will play a large role, just to name a few.

My work focuses on treating depression, anxiety, women's issues, and grief for adults and seniors. I also help with life transitions, .

I offer specialized services to family caregivers including consuting, counseling, and resources.
