Home

Hello and welcome! At Sharon Boris, LCSW, LLC, I am pleased to offer you a supportive and collaborative environment.

Choose from a wide variety of time tested methods, and we will work together to custom tailor a plan suited to your individual needs. It's all up to you!

We can use one or a combination of approaches. Whatever suits you best.

About

Sharon Boris received a BS degree Magna Cum Laude with a double major in biology and psychology and went on to receive a Master of Social Work degree from Loyola University Chicago.

Ms. Boris has been practicing for nearly 20 years. She has a wide variety of experience and knowledge that she continually applies to her work. Sharon Boris has worked in community counseling, teaching hospitals, holistic medical centers, and counseling programs.

Sharon makes use of the many different theoretical modalities that she has immersed herself in to put together the best plan for you.

Theories such as Psychodynamic Theory, Supportive Counseling, Cognitive Therapy, and Solution-focused approaches will play a large role in your counseling to help us decide which approach is best for you.
